# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participants are expected to act professionally and respectfully in project spaces. Technical disagreement is normal; personal attacks, harassment, and discriminatory behavior are not acceptable.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation details.
- Assume good intent unless behavior clearly shows otherwise.
- Protect confidential and sensitive information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or intimidation
- Personal insults
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code.
